Learn how Crate and Barrel created marketing videos without hiring a videographer

Even big brands can have trouble turning out marketing videos quickly. Crate and Barrel’s marketing team wanted to create a video promoting the spring catalog, but didn’t have the time—or budget—to hire a videographer.

So they used Animoto instead. Crate and Barrel’s team took photos from the catalog and turned out a social-ready Animoto video in just one day. The marketing video drove traffic on both Facebook and Instagram, getting significant impressions, clicks, and shares on both platforms.
